The arc of the work

From an emergency COVID-19 response to a financed, lasting state of preparedness.

Where it began

C19RM resourced an urgent national response: oxygen, diagnostics, supply chains and frontline capacity, delivered at speed across the federation.

What it leaves behind

Plants, cold chain, warehouses and revitalised primary care that now anchor Nigeria's standing preparedness, read against JEE, IHR and the NAPHS.
